Hardware Debugging Competition

A 3-round online hardware debugging competition designed to test students' theoretical knowledge and practical circuit troubleshooting skills. Participants progress from basic quizzes to hands-on debugging of complex circuits using Tinkercad, applying systematic approaches to identify and fix faults.
