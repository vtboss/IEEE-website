Empowering Women in Engineering!

The NKSS WiE Congress is a platform designed to empower and inspire women in engineering. Event Date: 25th March 2024

Event Time: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM

What to Expect: Keynote addresses, leadership workshops, networking opportunities

HACK-N-HUNT

Unleash Your Hacking Skills!

HACK-N-HUNT is a thrilling competition that challenges participants to discover and exploit vulnerabilities in a controlled, simulated environment. The event promotes collaboration, sharpens technical skills, and encourages real-world problem-solving, providing a great opportunity for budding cybersecurity professionals to showcase their expertise.

Event Date: 5th April 2024

Event Time: 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM

Event Venue: Computer Labs, S.G. Balekundri Institute of Technology

What to Expect: Hands-on experience in ethical hacking, cybersecurity challenges, collaboration

Sharpen your cybersecurity skills, test your problem-solving abilities, and learn new techniques to identify and secure vulnerabilities. This competition is ideal for those looking to enhance their technical expertise and stand out in the tech community!
