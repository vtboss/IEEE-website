Hack N Hunt 2.0

Date: 29/10/2025 | Time: 2:00 PM onwards

Decode. Discover. Dominate.

Rules & Regulations

General Rules ▶
  • Team Size: 3 members per team.
  • Eligibility: Open to all SGBIT students.
  • Accepted Languages: C, C++, Java, Python.
  • Registration Fee:
    • IEEE Members (all members must be IEEE): ₹30/team
    • Non-IEEE Members: ₹79/team
  • All participants must register prior to the event.
  • Participants must carry their college ID cards.
Round 1: The Quiz ▶
  • The quiz will be conducted offline.
  • Mobile phones and any electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Any form of malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Teams must submit their answers before the time limit.
  • Top-performing teams will qualify for the final round.
Round 2: The Hunt ▶
  • The final round is a thrilling on-campus treasure hunt infused with coding challenges.
  • Destroying or damaging college property will result in immediate disqualification.
  • No use of mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic devices.
  • Teams must stay together throughout the event.
  • Participants must maintain decorum and discipline.
  • Respect all event coordinators and follow instructions carefully.
