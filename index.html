About

We are IEEE SGBIT Student Branch

The IEEE SGBIT Student Branch fosters innovation and professional growth among students in engineering and technology. It offers workshops, seminars, and networking opportunities, enhancing technical and managerial skills. Established in 2020, the branch encourages collaboration, outreach programs, and participation in competitions, contributing to personal and community development.
